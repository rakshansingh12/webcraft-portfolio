Welcome to My Project

This is the first paragraph inside our centered container. By setting the maximum width to 1200px and using auto margins, the content remains easy to read on large screens while automatically adjusting to fit smaller devices.

The padding inside the container ensures that the text doesn't touch the edges of the box, providing a clean and professional look. This is a common layout technique used in almost every modern website to keep content organized and focused.
